# Customer_Churn_Analysis
End-to-end Customer Churn Analysis using Python, SQL Server and Power BI.

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies as acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones.

This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies churn drivers, segments customers based on risk and revenue, and provides actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention and profitability.

The project follows an end-to-end analytics workflow using **Python**, **SQL Server**, and **Power BI**.


## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and service usage patterns.
- Identify major factors contributing to customer churn.
- Perform revenue and customer segmentation analysis.
- Identify high-risk customers for proactive retention.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.
- Simulate business scenarios using What-if analysis.

## 📌 Key Insights

- Overall customer churn rate is **26.54%**, meaning approximately **1 in every 4 customers** left the service.
- **Month-to-Month contract customers** have the highest churn compared to long-term contracts.
- **Fiber Optic customers** contribute the largest share of customer churn.
- **Electronic Check** customers show higher churn than other payment methods.
- **Los Angeles** generates the highest revenue among all cities.
- Over **2,500 customers** are classified as **High Risk**, requiring immediate retention efforts.
- Competitor offerings and customer support experience are among the leading churn reasons.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Convert Month-to-Month customers into long-term contract customers through discounts and loyalty programs.
- Prioritize retention campaigns for High-Risk and high CLTV customers.
- Improve customer support quality and service experience.
- Address major churn reasons identified in the root cause analysis.
- Focus retention initiatives on high revenue cities and customer segments.
- Use customer risk segmentation for proactive engagement strategies.
